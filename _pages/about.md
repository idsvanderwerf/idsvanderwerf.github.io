I am PhD candidate at the signal processing systems (SPS) group under supervison of [dr. Richard C. Hendriks](https://sps.ewi.tudelft.nl/People/bio.php?id=47) (TU Delft) and [Prof. dr. Richard Heusdens](https://sps.ewi.tudelft.nl/People/bio.php?id=48) (NLDA). My PhD project is a collaboration between the TU Delft, TNO (a dutch research institute) and the NLDA (Dutch defence academy).

My research interests are waveform and array design for wireless communications and sensing, with a special passion for underwater acoustic communications. More details can be found under the [research](https://idsvanderwerf.github.io/research) tab.

I received the MSc degree from TU Delft in April 2022, on the topic of gridless sound field reconstruction. This project was a collaboration with Bang & Olufsen.
