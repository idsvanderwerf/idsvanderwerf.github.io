I received the MSc degree from TU Delft in April 2022, on the topic of gridless sound field reconstruction. Starting in August 2022, I have been a PhD student at the signal processing systems (SPS) group with [dr. Richard C. Hendriks](https://sps.ewi.tudelft.nl/People/bio.php?id=47) and [Prof. dr. Richard Heusdens](https://sps.ewi.tudelft.nl/People/bio.php?id=48), working on a project about underwater communications, more specifically about creating a high bandwidth, doppler tolerant communication link. The project is a collaboration between TNO (a dutch research institute), the NLDA (Dutch defence academy) and the TU Delft.
